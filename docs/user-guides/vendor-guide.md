## Getting Started

### Vendor Registration

1. **Access Vendor Portal**
   - Visit `/auth/signup`
   - Select "Vendor" as your role
   - Click "Sign Up" to begin registration

2. **Complete Registration Form**
   ```
   Personal Information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Role in Business
   
   Business Information:
   - Business Name
   - Business Type
   - Years in Business
   - Number of Locations
   ```

3. **Business Documents**
   - Upload required documents:
     - Business License
     - Tax Identification Number
     - Food Service Permit
     - Insurance Certificate
     - Health Department Permit

### Account Approval Process

1. **Document Review**
   - All documents verified by admin team
   - Business information validated
   - Legal compliance confirmed

2. **Timeline**
   - Review process: 3-5 business days
   - You'll receive email updates
   - Approval required before going live

3. **Post-Approval**
   - Account activation email
   - Access to vendor dashboard
   - Complete restaurant setup process

## Menu Management

### Adding Menu Items

1. **Access Menu Manager**
   - Go to `/dashboard/restaurants`
   - Select your restaurant
   - Click "Manage Menu"

2. **Create New Item**
   - Click "Add Menu Item"
   - Fill required information:
     ```
     Item Details:
     - Item Name
     - Description
     - Price
     - Category
     
     Visual Content:
     - Item Image
     - Nutrition Information (optional)
     
     Customization:
     - Size Options
     - Add-on Options
     - Preparation Instructions
     ```

3. **Image Requirements**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Order Management Problems
- **Orders not appearing**: Check internet connection, refresh dashboard
- **Payment issues**: Verify payment gateway status, contact support
- **Customer complaints**: Follow escalation process, document incidents
- **Delivery delays**: Communicate with customer, update status

#### Menu Issues
- **Items not showing**: Check availability status, verify category
- **Price discrepancies**: Update menu immediately, communicate changes
- **Image problems**: Upload new images, check file formats
- **Out of stock**: Mark items unavailable, update inventory

#### Technical Issues
- **Dashboard not loading**: Clear browser cache, try different browser
- **Login problems**: Reset password, check account status
- **Mobile app issues**: Update to latest version, reinstall if needed
- **Payment gateway errors**: Contact payment processor, verify account

### Getting Support

#### Support Channels
- **Vendor Support Portal**: `/dashboard/support`
- **Phone Support**: (800) 123-VEND
- **Email Support**: vendors@fooddelivery.com
- **Live Chat**: Available 9 AM - 9 PM

#### Escalation Process
1. **Level 1**: General questions and support
2. **Level 2**: Technical issues and account problems
3. **Level 3**: Complex business matters and escalations
4. **Executive Support**: Critical business issues
